Artist Biography

Growing up in the Philippines, being inspired by cartoons, anime, music and video games, Fiel Aquino is graphic designer studying at the University of New Haven who is interested in motion graphics, digital illustration, and digital fabrication. He has illustrated objects from the Avengers series, as well as anime characters like Gohan, from Dragon Ball Z Since moving back to the United States, he dove deeper into the realm of music, which grabbed his heart instantly. Something about the lyrics and the mood influences the way he thinks about art and generates ideas. Created laser cuts like llamas, making a reference to Fortnite llamas, as well as a plaque that uses a crest and replaced all the symbols to make it about himself. Each piece of work hitting his interests.

Fiel was first intrigued by graphic design in ninth grade. One of the main ideas that got him was the availability to be flexible with the creativity. He decided to pursue that career path after taking a course for a whole year dedicated to it. Fiel first became interested in motion graphics when he was shown the capability it can go to in sophomore year in one of his classes, then again getting a bigger interest in his junior year of college. During his junior year of college, he learned how to digital illustrate using a tablet, as well as learning how to laser cut, 3d print and etching. Fiel is currently expanding his knowledge at the university as well as improving his portfolio as a designer. Fiel sees himself working for a digital illustration or fabrication company in the big apple, New York City.
